📊 About NHTSA Data

National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) is a U.S. government agency responsible for reducing deaths, injuries, and economic losses from motor vehicle crashes.

Data Sources: NHTSA collects data through crash tests, consumer complaints, manufacturer reports, and field investigations. All data is publicly accessible via their API.

How to Use: Enter a vehicle's year, make, and model above to view comprehensive safety information including crash test ratings, recalls, consumer complaints, and manufacturer communications.
